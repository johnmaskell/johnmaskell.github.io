About

JM Coastal LTD was established in 2016 as a specialist hydrodynamic modelling consultancy with particular expertise in developing coastal models. With a significant percentage of the world's population living close to the coast, continuing coastal development and future challenges such as sea level rise, coastal science continues to be a relevant and exciting field in which to work. Coastal modelling is an increasingly valuable tool for making informed and sustainable decisions for coastal planning. With a diverse background in coastal modelling, both as an academic, and in the private sector as a catastrophe modeller, I hope that through JM Coastal LTD I can bring my passion to the craft of developing accurate and robust hydrodynamic models to meet some of the challenges that this diverse and dynamic environment presents.

John Maskell Bsc, MSc, PhD
JM Coastal LTD
