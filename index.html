About

We specialize in carrying out a range of coastal and estuarine modelling projects including tides, surges, waves, defence breaching and overtopping, coastal flooding and sediment transport. We have the ability to utilise a range of modelling tools, software and data do deliver accurate coastal modelling solutions.

Sediment Transport

Sediment transport modelling is used to predict morphological development of coastal regions and estuaries due to wave and tidal currents and can aid the design of beach preservation schemes, predict transport regime changes due to structures and assist operational dredging.

Coastal Flooding

Coastal flooding can have devastating consequences. Modelling coastal flooding can aid the design of flood protection schemes, analyse the potential impact due realistic scenarious and investgate the exposure of high value infrastructure.
